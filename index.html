Services

We Do The Best To Serve You!

As an Underwriters Laboratories Listed installing company, San Francisco Fire Protection,
Inc designs and installs fire life safety alarm systems in accordance with the current National Fire Protection Association.
We also provide inspections as required, with maintenance and service for our clients.

Service Provided:

Design and Installation for Fire Alarm Systems

  • CAD Drawings
    • Plan & Permits Submittals
    • Inspection with the Local Authority Having Jurisdiction for approval
  • 24hrs Monitoring Service with a U/L listed Central Station
  • 24hrs response for Emergency Services
  • Maintenance Service for Fire Alarm System
    • Quarterly, semi-annually and annually testing to meet NFPA codes and requirements
    • Response to fire alarm system troubles
    • Certification for Local or Monitored Fire Alarm System
  • Repair for Fire Alarm related equipments
  • Upgrade of existing fire alarm systems as required

About Us

San Francisco Fire Protection, Inc. is an Underwriters Laboratories listed fire alarm installation and service company.
As a minority operated and owned company, San Francisco Fire Protection, Inc. has been installing and servicing fire alarm
systems in the greater Bay Area since its conception in April 2001.

Employment

Positions

Full time field technicians to perform fire alarm installation, inspection, testing, maintenance, and service.

Requirements

Previous experience in a related industry is preferred but not a must. On-site training is available with a journey man technician for the first
time starter in this industry. Fire Alarm is a property and life safety service; therefore emergency service and quick response will be needed
(may be dispatched 24 hours and 7 days a week.) Must be willing to travel to respond to customers needs. Good driving record is required.

Compensation:

Hourly w/OT applicable and benefits package for full time personnel. Company vehicle and Nextel phone
are provided with allowance for personal use.
